DevOps Engineer

Remote
Full-time
Engineering

Join our DevOps team to build and maintain our CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, and cloud environments to support our growing client base.

About the Role

As a DevOps Engineer at DexaMinds, you'll be responsible for building and maintaining the infrastructure and deployment pipelines that support our applications. You'll work closely with development teams to implement DevOps best practices, automate processes, and ensure the reliability and scalability of our systems.

Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and maintain CI/CD pipelines
  • Automate infrastructure provisioning and configuration using IaC tools
  • Monitor system performance and troubleshoot issues
  • Implement security best practices and ensure compliance
  • Collaborate with development teams to improve deployment processes
  • Document infrastructure and operational procedures

Requirements

  • 3+ years of experience in DevOps or SRE roles
  • Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, and containerization
  • Proficiency with infrastructure as code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
  • Knowledge of CI/CD tools and practices
  • Strong scripting skills (Bash, Python)
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
  • Understanding of networking and security concepts

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana)
  • Knowledge of database administration
  • Experience with microservices architecture
  • Familiarity with agile development methodologies
  • Relevant certifications (AWS, Azure, Kubernetes)

Our Hiring Process

  1. Application Review: Our recruitment team will review your application and resume.
  2. Initial Interview: If your profile matches our requirements, we'll schedule an initial interview.
  3. Technical Assessment: You'll complete a technical assessment relevant to the role.
  4. Team Interview: Meet with potential team members and leaders.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: If selected, we'll extend an offer and work with you on a smooth onboarding.
