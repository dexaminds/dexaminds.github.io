Our Hiring Process

We've designed a thoughtful process to find the right candidates while giving you insight into our company and culture.

1

Application Review

Our recruitment team carefully reviews your application, resume, and portfolio to assess your qualifications and experience relative to the position requirements.

2

Initial Interview

If your profile matches our requirements, we'll schedule an initial interview to discuss your experience, skills, and career aspirations, as well as tell you more about DexaMinds and the role.

3

Technical Assessment

Depending on the role, you may be asked to complete a technical assessment, coding challenge, or design exercise to demonstrate your skills and problem-solving approach.

4

Team Interview

Meet with potential team members and leaders to dive deeper into technical discussions and assess cultural fit. This is also your opportunity to ask questions and get to know your future colleagues.

5

Offer & Onboarding

If you're selected, we'll extend an offer and work with you to ensure a smooth onboarding process. We're committed to setting you up for success from day one.
