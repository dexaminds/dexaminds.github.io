    🚀 DexaMinds Lead Generation Process

    📌 Overview

    This guide outlines DexaMinds' standardized lead generation process, ensuring consistency, efficiency, and high-quality pipeline development across all marketing and sales channels.

    🔍 Lead Sources

    🔸 Inbound Marketing

    • 🌐 Website contact forms
    • 📚 Content marketing (blogs, whitepapers, ebooks)
    • 🎥 Webinar registrations
    • 💬 Social media engagement
    • 🔎 SEO and organic search

    🔸 Outbound Marketing

    • ✉️ Email campaigns
    • 📞 Cold calling
    • 💼 LinkedIn outreach
    • 🏢 Industry events & conferences
    • 🤝 Partnerships and referrals

    ✅ Lead Qualification Process

    1️⃣ Lead Capture

    • All incoming leads are captured in the CRM system
    • Mandatory fields: Name, Company, Email, Phone, Source
    • Optional fields: Job Title, Company Size, Industry, Specific Needs

    2️⃣ Initial Qualification – MQL (Marketing Qualified Lead)

    • Lead scored based on:

    • 📊 Demographics

    • 📈 Online behavior
    • 📰 Content engagement
    • 🌐 Website interactions
    • ✅ Threshold: Minimum score of 60/100 to qualify as MQL

    3️⃣ Sales Qualification – SQL (Sales Qualified Lead)

    Handled by SDR (Sales Development Representative) using the BANT framework:

    • 💰 Budget: Does the lead have budget allocated?
    • 👤 Authority: Is the lead a decision maker or influencer?
    • 📌 Need: What problem(s) are they solving?
    • ⏳ Timeline: When are they planning to implement?

    🔄 Lead Handoff Process

    📤 MQL ➡️ SQL

    • 🔔 Auto-notification to assigned SDR
    • ⏱️ SLA: Respond within 24 hours
    • 📞 Schedule discovery call within 48 hours

    📈 SQL ➡️ Opportunity

    • 🖥️ Product demo scheduled
    • 📑 Customized solution presentation
    • 📝 Proposal preparation & submission

    🌱 Lead Nurturing Strategy

    • ✉️ Drip campaigns triggered by user behavior
    • 🎯 Personalized content recommendations
    • 🎟️ Webinar & event invitations
    • 🗂️ Share case studies & testimonials
    • 📆 Regular SDR follow-ups

    Team Responsibilities

    📞 SDR Initial outreach, BANT qualification, SQL handoff

    🤝 Customer Success Client onboarding and post-sale handoff

    • 🧹 Periodic data cleansing & validation
